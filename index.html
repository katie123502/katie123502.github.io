An Exploration of Human Herpesvirus Homology

From Team VALK

Vienna Huang, Anya Raju, Lucile Zhu, Katie Sie

This tool will allow you to visualize the 9 main strains of the human herpesvirus along with a few substrains and zoonotic strains.

Note on MSA view: The MSA plugin requires uploading files containing the results of the MSA alignment. You can find these files here, and you can download and upload them to the static page to visualize alignments on the MSA viewer. You can also view the MSA alignment files via the default linear genome view without downloading/uploading yourself.
